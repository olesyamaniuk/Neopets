About us

Neopets: Island Builders is a creative and interactive game where players help Neopets rebuild their island after a mysterious calamity. Design structures, gather resources, and explore the island's secrets while bonding with adorable Neopets. This engaging mix of simulation and adventure brings the charm of Neopia to your fingertips.
